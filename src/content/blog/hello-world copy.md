Starting something new is always a mix of excitement and uncertainty. You have this blank canvas in front of you and endless possibilities of what it could become — but also the very real chance that you'll stare at it for days without making a single mark.

## Why build a personal site?

I've been thinking about this for a while. Social media is great for quick updates, but it never really felt like *mine*. Everything exists on someone else's platform, shaped by someone else's algorithm. I wanted a space that I fully own — where the design, the content, and the vibe are all intentional.

So here we are. A blog, a gallery, and a little corner of the internet that's just for me.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Things I'm building** — web projects, tools, experiments
- **Things I'm learning** — frameworks, design patterns, random rabbit holes
- **Life stuff** — because code isn't everything, and the best ideas come from stepping away from the screen

## The stack

For the curious, this site is built with Astro, styled with Tailwind CSS, and has a sprinkle of Framer Motion for smooth animations. It's hosted on a tiny Hetzner VPS running Caddy. Simple, fast, and mine.

## Here we go

If you're reading this, thanks for stopping by. There's more to come — I promise to keep it real, keep it useful, and keep it human.

See you around. ✌️
